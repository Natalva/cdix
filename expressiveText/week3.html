laying on warm sand i stare into the enless blue space above my eyes
as it swallows me whole forgeting the world of skin and bones
Closing my eyes rays of light seep into my eye lids glowing red and orange
forgeting that winter ever existed, skin under the warm sun shifts to gold
as the sun picks me up and wraps her arms around me, healing and filling me with light
her light flows into my bloodstream Boiling inside as I land back onto the sand
staring into the ocean submerging myself in blue
bubbles of air surround me as i float to the surface
the world exists only through sensations, slowing down my thoughts
I feel the freshness of the water, my thoughts sink to the ocean floor burried under the sand
